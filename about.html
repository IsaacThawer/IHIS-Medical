Our Story

At Integrated Health Insurance Services LLC, we believe navigating health insurance should be simple and stress-free. After more than 25 years of experience in the insurance industry, We realized how overwhelming Medicare, Covered California, and other health programs can be for families—especially within the Hispanic community. That’s why we created a bilingual, community-first agency focused on education, trust, and personalized service. Our mission is to make sure you not only have the right coverage, but also understand it. This business was born out of a desire to help people protect what matters most: their health, their family, and their financial peace of mind.

Whether you’re turning 65, retiring, or exploring health insurance options for the first time, we’re here to guide you every step of the way. At Integrated Health Insurance Services LLC, you’re not just a policy number—you’re family. Let us help you find the coverage that fits your life and budget.
